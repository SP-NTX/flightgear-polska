FlightGear Polska 4th Anniversary


After months of preparations, countless hours of exhausting training, detailed planning, overcoming numerous challenges, and a large amount of teamwork, we are thrilled to officially announce the grand celebration of the 4th Anniversary of FlightGear Polska!

This milestone event marks four incredible years of shared passion, dedication, and an commitment to the FlightGear community. We’ve grown from a small group of enthusiasts into one of the largest and most active communities in the FlightGear world.

About our airshow pilots:


SP-LWP

He is known in our community as Parrott, joined FlightGear Polska in 2020 and quickly became one of the most important members of our community. In 2021, he founded the virtual airline called Parrott Airlines, which, after three years, remains the largest active airline in FlightGear. Since 2021, he has been an administrator on the Flightgear Polska Discord Server.

Parrott is also a collector of equipment from the Polish People's Army. His collection includes various types of gas masks, including the SzM41M with KF. He is one of the organizers of FGPL4, and his piloting skills will be showcased during our airshow.


SP-RVB

FlightGear Pilot since 2022, runs a Youtube channel with diverse content, including FlightGear. His facourite aircraft is the Airbus A320. He has been an administrator of FlightGear Polska Discord Server multiple times and has organized serval events, including "1 z 10".

During our airshow, SP-RVB will demonstrate the capabilities of the A320 during the Airbus Demo.


SP-3358

Also known as "Missed Approach", or simply 3358, is a pilot both in FlightGear and in real life, has been member of our comunity since 2024. He is a glider pilot in Aeroklub Ziemi Lubuskiej, making him the only current real-life pilot in our community

During our airshow, he will showcase his skills in the AZL Żelazny Demo, Helitow Demo and PZL Dromader Demo.


TIGER

Tiger, our friend from Slovakia, will showcase his helicopter piloting skills during our airshow flying aircraft such as the Bo105. He is one of the key members of our community, participating in nearly every event organized by FlightGear Polska


SP-LYNX

Also known as "janout", he is a pilot in FlightGear, XPlane 11, and on Vatsim. He is one of the most important members of our community, and a key organizer od the event celebrating the 4th anniversary of FlightGear Polska. He has been an FGPL administrator since 2022. Throughout his FlightGear carrer, he has completed hundreds of flights and served as an ATC. He is also an aviation enthusiast and a plane spotter. SP-LYNX will demonstrate his piloting skills during our airshow,, flying various aircraft such as the F16, Zivko Edge, and many others


SP-BFA

She has been a member of our community since 2024. Although she hasn't completed many flights in her FlightGear carrer, her skills have been recognized and appreciated by the event organizers. She participated in the preparations for FGPL4 airshow, assisting with displays such as the Kolibersparx. During our event, she will serve as a backup pilot and will fly as co-pilot in displays such as Kolibersparx and Cap10 Heart.


LOT87

He has been a member of our community since 2020, actively participating in many group flights and events. He has served as an administrator of FlightGear Polska for nearly 3 years. LOT87 is one of the few pilots in FLightGear who can fly the Space Shuttle accordding to the real life procedures, and his favourite aircraft is Concorde. During our airshow, he will showcase his piloting skills by landing the Space Shuttle, and performing barious other displays.


SP-BSK

Known in our community as Bysiek, has been a pilot in FlightGear, XPlane and MSFS since 2021. He is also a pilot on Vatsim network and ATC on IVAO. Bysiek has ran a YouTube channel focused on FlightGear and founded the cirtual airline, Perfromance Airlines (known as PerA). He will showcase his skills during our airshow celebrating the 4th anniversary of FlightGear Polska


VIPER04

Known simply as 04, is one of the most experienced fighter pilots in FlightGear, scoring over 400 kills during his 4 year FlightGear carrer. Although his real callsign remain unknown, he is well known for being very good pilot on F16 aircraft. He is member and co-founder of VAF, FGVAF and 9vELT. His first appearance in FlightGear was in November 2020, when he joined 9vELT. Since 2022 he flies alongside VIPER01, 02, 03,... 16, in VAF, defending Polish Airspace in FlightGear. During the event, he will demonstrate his piloting and air combat skills in the 9vELT demo, VAF demo, and WVR Demo.


LENAR

He has been a member of our community since 2023 and a pilot in FlightGear since 2022. During our airshow, he will showcase his skills with a B-1B and F16 demo

0001

0001 has been a member of our community since 2023 and has been flying in FlightGear since 2022. He is a plane spotter and, like 4054 and LENAR01, is mainly interested in military aviation. In FlightGear, he showcases great piloting skills with the F16, which he will demonstrate during our airshow celebrating the 4th anniversary of our community, FlightGear Polska.


SP-NTX

He is also known in our community as simply "ntx", sometimes called viper, has been flying in FlightGear since 2019. He is the founder of the FlightGear Polska community, a FlightGear developer, and co-creator of addons such as AnotherGUI and MPChatImprovements, and aircrafts, such as PZL Koliber Family, W-3 Sokół, top secret, top secret #2, Boeing 737 family, and Boeing 747 family. In FlightGear he has completed thousands of flights, summing up to more than 3500 hours in virtual skies, flying various aircraft tupes, from gliders to military jets and even space shuttles.

He is a plane spotter, with notable achievements like photographing the KC-10 Extender, F35, F15, DC-8, MD-11, and many more. He also wrote lyrics for the AVC, song made together with SP-LYNX and SP-LWP, and Nameless 7. His favourite sport is sailing, and he copetes in the ILCA class single-handed sailboats.

Additionally, he is a photographer specializing in astrophotography, both tracked and untracked, planets and deep sky. As one of the main orgganizers of the event celebrating the 4th anniversary of FlightGear Polska, he will showcase his piloting skills on various aircrafts, such as F16, F15, Zivko Edge, Cap10, PZL Koliber, and Lim-2


SP-KAD

KAD is a FlightGear Pilot since October 2023. He flies mainly F-16, sometimes he flies gliders, airliners, and general aviation aircrafts too. He is one of the most skilled pilots in FlightGear and one of the most active members of our server. He will showcase his skills during our airshow
